TrackMyMed - Never miss your meds again

Do you need to take medication on a regular basis? Do your staying healthy depend on your taking medicines on time? TrackMyMed app can help.

Medical science has advanced a lot. We can today manage many life threatening conditions which would have been fatal just a few decades ago. Asthma, diebetes, organ failure still require lifelong management, but are no longer life threatening.

If you or your loved one need to make sure they are taking their medicines on time, our app can help. No matter whether you are a patient or a care provider, our app can help you make sure you never miss a single dosage.

The App

Features

  • Get alerted when the medicine should be taken.
  • See the name of the medicine and the dosage in the alert.
  • Add as many medicines and timings you need.
  • Care providers can choose to get alerted when you take a medicine.
  • Care provider will be alerted if you miss a medication.
  • Track your medicine adherence report
